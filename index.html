Research Objectives

MEMS resonators have amazing capabilities and versatality to applications in various fields. At Microsystems Lab, we are working to improve the Q-factor of MEMS devices by understanding the physical intricacies from ground up and find solutions to overcome or subdue them.

Research Summaries

  • Bio-inspired zero power cochlear implant
  • VO2 Microwire Resonators
  • Superharmonic Resonant Response Method
  • Suspended Microchannel Resonators
  • Custom circuit design for MEMS
  • Energy Loss Estimation
  • Phononic Crystals
  • Shape Optimization using ML
  • Akhieser Damping in MEMS Resonators
